College of Criminal Justice Education

The criminology program aims to develop well-rounded individuals by providing a broad, holistic education that emphasizes not only technical skills but also ethical values, creativity, and leadership. Key goals include fostering critical thinking and competitiveness to address real-world issues, cultivating leadership skills for environmental stewardship, and nurturing a commitment to truth, integrity, and social responsibility. The program prepares students to contribute positively to their families, communities, and the nation by equipping them with the knowledge, attitudes, and habits necessary to uplift society and advocate for justice and sustainability.

College of Law

The law program aims to instill in students a deep understanding of the law, fairness, and equality, preparing them for legal practice while emphasizing respect for the rule of law and professional ethics. It fosters social responsibility to protect civil liberties, promotes leadership aligned with the needs of the Filipino people, and encourages active participation in shaping a society that upholds human dignity and freedom.

Institute of Agricultural Technology

The Institute of Agricultural Technology aims to produce skilled graduates to meet regional workforce needs, create and maintain valuable agricultural resources for instructional development, empower farmers and other stakeholders with enhanced knowledge and productivity, and actively engage in research and community-focused development programs.

Polytechnic School

The Polytechnic School aims to provide a strong foundation in the latest technological advancements, develop essential technical skills, safety practices, and workplace ethics, foster a positive disposition in interactions with others, ensure excellence and competence in task performance, and encourage perseverance and diligence in students' careers.

School of Arts and Sciences

The School of Arts and Sciences aims to provide students with a broad, well-rounded education, equipping them with critical thinking skills, creativity, and the ability to adapt to an ever-changing world. Its programs prepare students for a wide range of career opportunities in the arts, sciences, and humanities.
